THE LIFE AND WORKS OF JOSE RIZAL

GE 9(2018)-B

About


This compilation features various statues, busts, markers, streets, and parks around the world dedicated to Dr. Jose Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines. It highlights how different countries honor his legacy through public monuments that symbolize his fight for freedom, education, and national pride. From Asia to Europe and the Americas, these tributes reflect the global recognition of Rizals influence and ideals. Together, they serve as powerful reminders of his lasting impact not just on Filipino identity but on the universal struggle for justice, human dignity, and peaceful reform.
